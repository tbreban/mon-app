# Supporting your ADP migration, from start to finish

**The premium Business Project Management specialisation for ADP projects - by practitioners who know the solution from the inside.**

Migrating to a new ADP version (Decidium, ADP Link, GlobalView, GAP, ACE…) is not a standard HRIS project. The intricacies of ADP configuration, the richness of its modules, its proprietary interfaces and the rigour expected by the vendor all require a Business Project Manager who speaks **the same language** as the ADP teams - while remaining fully independent of them.

GBA Connect leads the Business Project Management of your ADP projects with **in-depth knowledge of the solution**, built up across dozens of assignments carried out by the founding partners before the firm was established.

## Who this offer is for

- You are migrating from one ADP version to another (V6000, ADP Link, GlobalView, etc.) and want to secure the migration path
- You are implementing ADP for the first time and need an independent business reference alongside the ADP teams
- You have been through an ADP migration that went poorly and need to stabilise the situation
- You manage several entities on different ADP versions and are looking to consolidate onto a single platform

## What we deliver

- **Project scoping tailored to ADP**: scope, governance, schedule aligned with vendor milestones, internal workload plan
- **Functional specifications** written in ADP terminology: pay elements, populations, profiles, triggers, standard interfaces
- **ADP team management**: deliverable tracking, quality control, gap and change management
- **Cutover strategy**: migration plan, rollback scenarios, pre/post controls, parallel run where applicable
- **Business functional testing** on real and complex cases: collective agreements, atypical populations, rare but critical events
- **Monitoring of the first post-go-live payroll runs**: variance control, team support, incident management

## Success indicators

- **Go-live delivered** on the ADP schedule, without contractual overrun
- **First post-migration payroll run** consistent with the reference payrolls (parallel run validated)
- **DSN submissions processed** without blocking rejections from the very first month
- **Payroll teams autonomous** on the new version by the end of the assignment

## Format & duration

A long-term assignment, generally between **6 and 18 months** depending on the scale of the migration and the number of entities. Time & materials or fixed price per phase.
