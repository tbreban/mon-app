# Sick Leave & Annual Leave: securing your compliance after the 2024 reform

**Annual leave accrual during sick leave, retroactivity, valuation: a legal and payroll issue that can no longer be ignored.**

The Act of 22 April 2024, which transposes European case law on the accrual of paid annual leave during sick leave absences, has turned a peripheral topic into a **major risk area for the payroll function**. Many companies have not yet applied the reform in full, have not addressed retroactivity for prior periods, and remain exposed to both individual and collective disputes.

GBA Connect helps you **achieve compliance, secure retroactive adjustments, and sustain the correct treatment** in your ongoing payroll.

## Who this offer is for

- You have **not yet applied** the 22 April 2024 reform and need to bring your organisation into compliance
- You have applied it but have **not addressed retroactivity** for prior periods
- You have **individual disputes** or **employee requests** that you are unable to qualify
- You want to **establish your company's formal policy** on the accrual and valuation of annual leave during sick leave

## What we deliver

- **Diagnosis of your situation**: periods affected, impacted employee populations, volumes, financial exposure
- **Management policy**: accrual rules (occupational illness, non-occupational illness, workplace accidents), leave-taking, carry-over, and valuation rules
- **Retroactivity treatment**: scope, calculation methodology, regularisation modalities (current payroll, final settlement statements, former employees)
- **Payroll configuration**: adaptation of rules in your system (ADP, Cegid, Silae, others) or validation of the vendor's standard configuration
- **Internal communications**: talking points for managers, employees, and employee representatives
- **Ongoing processing safeguards**: procedures, controls, and watchpoints for future payroll runs
