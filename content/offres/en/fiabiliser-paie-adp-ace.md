# Securing your ADP payroll with the ACE control tool

**Detecting discrepancies before they become incidents - payslip by payslip, month after month.**

An ADP payroll typically contains several thousand active pay codes, configurations inherited over many years, and a volume of payslips that no payroll administrator can exhaustively check by hand. Discrepancies always exist - the question is not *whether* they exist, but *when* you discover them: before the DSN filing, after an employee complaint, or during an URSSAF inspection.

GBA Connect uses **ACE, ADP's payroll control tool**, to transform this control process into a systematic, traceable, and industrialised workflow.

## Who this offer is for

- You are an ADP payroll user (Decidium, GlobalView, ADP Link, GAP) and your payslip volume exceeds the manual control capacity of your teams
- You have just completed a payroll migration or a major configuration change and want to secure the first payroll runs
- Your current controls rely on manual Excel extractions that are not replayable or traceable
- You hold payroll compliance responsibility (CFO, HR Director, Payroll Manager) and need auditable proof of control

## What we deliver

- **Mapping of your payroll risk areas**: sensitive populations, high-stakes pay codes, high error-rate management events
- **Design and configuration of ACE controls**: business rules, tolerance thresholds, month-on-month and period-on-period controls
- **Monthly production of control reports**: discrepancies detected, root cause analysis, prioritisation of corrections
- **Remediation process management**: transmission to payroll administrators, correction tracking, clearance verification
- **Management reporting** for HR Directors/CFOs: discrepancy rate, recurring error typology, improvement indicators month after month
- **Auditable documentation and audit trail** in the event of an URSSAF inspection, statutory audit, or employee dispute

## Our approach

> *"Previously, we discovered discrepancies through employee feedback or N+1 retroactive adjustments. With the tooled control process in place, we detect them at the pre-payroll stage and correct them before the payslip is issued. DSN filings go through without rejection, and employee queries have dropped significantly."*

## Success indicators

- **Payslip control rate** brought to 100% (vs. sample-based control)
- **Discrepancy detection lead time** reduced to D-2 before payroll close (vs. post-payslip detection)
- **Reduction in retroactive adjustments** month on month through identification of recurring root causes
- **Evidence file** structured and ready to mobilise in the event of an external inspection

## Format & duration

Two possible modalities depending on your maturity level:

- **Implementation + handover**: an 8 to 12 week project to map, configure, and train your teams - you then operate independently.
- **Delegated monthly run**: GBA Connect produces the ACE controls and associated reporting each month. Annual commitment, volume billed according to number of payslips and configuration complexity.

## What we do not do

GBA Connect does **not handle payroll production** itself: we control, we do not input. This separation is deliberate - it guarantees the independence of the control process from production.
