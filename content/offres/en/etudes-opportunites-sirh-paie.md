# Assessing an HRIS or Payroll Opportunity Before Committing to the Project

**Deciding to go — or not to go — on the basis of a solid business case, not a gut feeling.**

Before launching a major transformation project (HRIS overhaul, payroll insourcing, multi-country consolidation, post-acquisition harmonisation), it is rare to have a clear picture of the expected value, total costs, and real risks involved. The decision is often made under the pressure of a committee meeting, without a substantiated opportunity dossier.

GBA Connect builds with you **a costed, argued, and defensible opportunity dossier** for your Executive Committee, Management Committee, or shareholders.

## Who this offer is for

- You are considering a large-scale HRIS/Payroll transformation and need to make the case to your executive management or shareholders
- You have several scenarios on the table (stay the course, evolve, overhaul) and need to choose between them
- You are leading an insourcing, outsourcing, or payroll operating model change
- You are integrating new entities and need to decide on tool convergence

## What we deliver

- **Mapping of the current state**: tools, processes, total costs (licences, ongoing operations, internal resources), quantified pain points
- **Costed scenarios**: 2 to 4 technical and organisational options, with high-level budget assumptions and an indicative timeline
- **Comparative analysis**: investment costs, recurring costs, expected benefits, risks per scenario, conditions for success
- **Argued business case**: return on investment, payback period, sensitivity to assumptions
- **Ready-to-use decision dossier** for the Executive Committee, Management Committee or board, with a reasoned recommendation
- **Short-term action plan** if the decision is approved: next steps, resources to mobilise, critical milestones

## Success indicators

- A **decision that has been made**, documented, defensible, and traceable
- **Scenarios built on comparable assumptions**, not on vendors' claims
- A **business case** whose assumptions survive the implementation phase
- An **aligned team** — HR, Finance, IT, and General Management — on the path forward

## Format & duration

A scoped engagement, typically between **6 and 10 weeks** depending on the number of scenarios to assess and the availability of internal data. Fixed fee.
