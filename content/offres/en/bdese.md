# Designing and populating your BDESE

**A BDESE that does not serve social dialogue serves no purpose — and every update costs more than it contributes.**

The BDESE (Economic, Social and Environmental Database — formerly BDES) has become an extended legal obligation, but above all a **structuring tool for social dialogue**. Too often it is treated as an administrative chore: a heavy spreadsheet to produce, one that fails to reflect the reality of the business and serves neither employee representatives nor management.

GBA Connect designs and deploys a BDESE that **meets the legal obligation, feeds social dialogue, and remains maintainable over time** — tailored to your size, your sector, and your level of social maturity.

## Who this offer is for

- You are setting up your **first BDESE** and need a structure suited to your organisation
- Your existing BDESE is **unwieldy, hard to read, poorly maintained**, and you want to rebuild it
- You are integrating the **environmental dimension** (the "E" in BDESE) and have no methodology to do so
- You are changing your HRIS/Payroll system and want to **automate the BDESE feed** from your source data

## What we deliver

- **Audit of your current BDESE** or of your target obligation: compliance, readability, actual usefulness
- **Framework design**: adaptation to headcount (below or above 300 employees), sector, company agreements, and the maturity of social dialogue
- **Integration of the environmental component**: indicators suited to your activity, data sources, collection methodology
- **Population methodology**: extractions from HRIS/payroll, finance, and CSR systems; automation of data flows where possible
- **Update procedure**: calendar, responsibilities, consistency checks, validation before publication
- **Training for contributors and employee representatives**: understanding the framework, reading the data, using it in consultation processes
