# Securing your payroll data migration

**Migration succeeds or fails at the data migration stage - and the outcome is visible from the very first payslip.**

A payroll data migration is not simply a matter of mapping fields from one system to another. It means **reconstructing a social history** (accumulated leave balances, RTT balances, seniority, accrued entitlements, in-progress events, payments already made) in a form that the new system can correctly interpret, calculate, and report. The slightest error surfaces on the first payslip - and is paid for in adjustment runs, employee complaints, and lost team confidence.

GBA Connect manages your payroll data migration as **a project in its own right**, with a proven methodology and an enforceable quality standard.

## Who this offer is for

- You are running an HRIS/Payroll migration and data migration is a major project risk
- You are managing a merger or acquisition and need to consolidate multiple legacy payrolls
- You are insourcing or outsourcing your payroll and need to recover history from your current provider
- You have experienced a failed migration and need to consolidate the historical record retrospectively

## What we deliver

- **Migration strategy**: scope, depth of history, granularity, alternative scenarios based on constraints
- **Migration specifications**: source tables, target tables, transformation rules, handling of edge cases
- **Migration control plan**: integrity checks (volume, completeness, consistency), business checks (balances, accruals, entitlements)
- **Migration operations management**: oversight of technical workstreams, successive test runs, gap management
- **Business validation of migrations**: review with payroll managers, sign-off on migration acceptance reports by batch
- **Migration file** suitable for archiving and audit trail: full traceability of all transformations and residual discrepancies
