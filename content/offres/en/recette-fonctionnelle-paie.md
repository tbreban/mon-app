# Test your payroll before it tests your employees

**UAT is the last line of defence before go-live - it cannot be delegated to a consultant who is discovering your payroll for the first time.**

Successful payroll UAT is neither an administrative sign-off exercise nor a rubber-stamp before cutover. It is **the final test of truth** between the integrator's configuration and the reality of your business rules. Poorly conducted testing means discrepancies that surface on live payslips, employee complaints, and a payroll team whose confidence erodes from the very first month.

GBA Connect designs, executes, and challenges the testing of your payroll projects based on **your real cases - the most complex, the rarest, the highest-risk**.

## Who this offer is for

- You are entering the testing phase and the integrator is proposing their own test scripts - with no knowledge of your specific requirements
- You want an objective assessment of configuration quality before go-live, independent of the integrator
- Your payroll team does not have the time or the methodology to manage a structured testing campaign
- You have experienced a difficult go-live and need to consolidate the reliability of the configuration retrospectively

## What we deliver

- **Testing strategy**: scope, depth, critical scenarios, sensitive employee populations, milestones
- **Detailed test plans**: standard cases, complex cases, rare cases, edge cases - built around your actual business situations
- **UAT datasets**: representative employee populations, high-stakes events, specific parameters
- **Testing campaign execution**: cycle management, gap analysis, anomaly qualification, remediation tracking
- **Non-regression testing**: proof of stability after each integrator release
- **UAT acceptance reports** signed, traceable, and enforceable against the integrator

## Success indicators

- **Test coverage rate** for business rules and employee populations met within the test plan
- **Critical anomalies** detected and resolved before go-live, not after
- **UAT acceptance reports signed** across the full scope
- **First live payroll run** consistent with UAT results

## Format & duration

A scoped engagement, typically between **8 and 16 weeks** depending on scope, aligned with project milestones.
