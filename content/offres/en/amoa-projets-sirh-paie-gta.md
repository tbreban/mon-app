# Business Project Management for HRIS, Payroll and Time & Attendance Projects

**Driving your project from the business side — not just the project management side.**

Standard Business Project Management (AMOA) knows how to run a project: workshops, steering committees, schedules, deliverables. But on an HRIS or Payroll project, **the complexity is business complexity before it is project complexity**. A Business Project Manager who doesn't speak payroll will miss the misconfigured pay element, the unstable accounting interface, the incomplete cutover scenario — all hidden costs that only surface after go-live.

GBA Connect leads the Business Project Management of your HRIS/Payroll/Time & Attendance projects with a **dual requirement: methodological expertise and business expertise**, regardless of the vendor selected.

## Who this offer is for

- You are launching an HRIS, payroll or Time & Attendance overhaul project and need a Business Project Manager capable of challenging the system integrator on substance
- You want to focus on business steering and delegate the operational running of the project
- You already have a project team but need payroll expertise to validate configurations and functional testing
- You want to secure a cutover (go-live) in a high-stakes social or financial environment

## What we deliver

- **Project scoping**: scope, governance, schedule, internal workload, communication plan
- **Drafting and arbitration of functional specifications**: business rules, edge cases, atypical populations
- **Integrator management**: commitment tracking, deliverable quality control, change management
- **Design and execution of the functional testing strategy**: test plan, data sets, business scenarios, user acceptance testing
- **Cutover preparation and execution**: go-live checklist, rollback plan, monitoring of the first post-go-live payroll runs
- **Change management** with payroll, HR, manager and employee teams

## Our approach

> *"We had a Business Project Manager who asked the right questions of the integrator — one who wouldn't be won over by 'it'll work, trust us.' On sensitive pay elements, they demanded proof of configuration before functional testing, not during. The result: no unpleasant surprises at go-live."*
>
> — *Payroll Manager, multi-site industrial mid-sized company — assignment carried out by the founding partners prior to the creation of GBA Connect*

## Success indicators

- **Go-live delivered** on schedule and within budget
- **First post-go-live payroll run** with no DSN rejections and no large-scale corrections
- **User teams fully operational** from day one, without prolonged dependency on the integrator
- **Complete functional testing file** archived and enforceable against the integrator

## Format & duration

A long-term assignment, generally between **6 and 18 months** depending on the scale of the project. Time & materials or fixed price per phase (scoping, design, functional testing, cutover).

## What we do not do

GBA Connect is **not a system integrator** and does not perform tool configuration. Our role is to steer, specify, control and test — representing your business interests, which are distinct from those of the integrator.
